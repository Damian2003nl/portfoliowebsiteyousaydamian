Character Animation Series

A whimsical adventure featuring original characters

Project Details

This series was created using a combination of hand-drawn animation in Photoshop and compositing in After Effects. Each 30-second episode took approximately 80 hours to complete from concept to final render.

The characters were designed to appeal to both children and adults, with exaggerated expressions and fluid movements that bring their personalities to life.

3D Product Visualization

Photorealistic animations for commercial clients

Technical Approach

Created in Blender and Cinema 4D, these product animations feature advanced material shaders and lighting setups to achieve photorealism. Each project begins with detailed modeling based on technical specifications, followed by texture painting and rigging for any moving parts.

Creative Process

This experimental piece combines generative algorithms with hand-animated elements. The project explores the relationship between organic forms and digital creation, using particle systems and fluid simulations to create emergent behaviors.
